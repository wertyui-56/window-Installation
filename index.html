Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I need new windows?

Signs you may need new windows include: drafts or difficulty maintaining consistent temperatures, condensation between glass panes, difficulty opening or closing windows, noticeable exterior damage or decay, and increasing energy bills. Our experts can assess your windows during a free consultation.

What's the average cost of window replacement?

Window replacement costs vary based on the type, size, material, and number of windows. On average, homeowners spend between $300-$1,000 per window installed, with premium options reaching $1,500 or more. We offer financing options and can provide an accurate estimate after evaluating your specific needs.

How long does window installation typically take?

Most standard window replacements take about 30-60 minutes per window. A typical whole-house installation (10-15 windows) can be completed in 1-2 days. Custom or complex installations may take longer. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.

What window materials do you recommend?

The best material depends on your needs: vinyl offers excellent insulation with low maintenance; wood provides classic beauty but requires more upkeep; fiberglass combines durability with energy efficiency; aluminum is strong and lightweight. Our consultants will help you choose based on your climate, budget, and aesthetic preferences.
